About Molly Stoner

An elementary teacher for over 30 years, Molly Stoner brings to her work a vast experience with guiding and observing students, as well as an intuitive, warm presence. She has a B.A. in Psychology from Carleton College, an M.Ed. and elementary certification from Antioch/New England Graduate School, and extensive post-graduate work in education. She has been practicing yoga for over two decades and was certified by Hits the Spot Yoga (200- and 300-hour) and Breathe for Change (200-hour).

Molly's vision is to offer others the calm centering that the ongoing practice of yoga has offered her. “I hope to provide opportunities for renewal, equanimity and spontaneous joy that yoga offers.” Particularly interested in supporting fellow teachers, Molly encourages the development of each individual's inner resources through the practices of asana poses, meditation, breathwork and communication skills.

Location

Intuit Yoga and Educational Services is located on a 19 acre wooded hillside in Dummerston, Vermont. With extensive gardens, a babbling brook, and gentle vistas, it offers a feeling of renewal immediately upon arrival. Classes are held in a 25’ yurt, nestled between the gardens and woods. After classes, students are welcome to enjoy a cup of tea, take a walk in the woods, or sit and enjoy the quiet environment.
